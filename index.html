About

This club is meant for people who want to stay in shape and be healthy in a timely manner. We know, as students of Stuy, it is hard to find the time to manage your school work and your extra-curricular activities so the purpose of this club is to keep you active but not add another burden to your work load. We meet twice a week (Monday and Wednesday) on the first floor and we do various activities, not limited to but consist of the: one mile challenge, thirty minutes of constant running, and ab workouts. These workouts are along the Hudson River, Pier 25, and in school.
